STRONGS NT 5190: Τυχικός

Τυχικός (so WH; Winers Grammar, § 6, 1 l.) but R G L T Tr Τυχικός (Lipsius, Gram. Unters., p. 30; (Tdf. Proleg., p. 103; Chandler § 266)), τυχικοῦ, ὁ, Tychicus, an Asiatic Christian, friend and companion of the apostle Paul: Acts 20:4; Ephesians 6:21; Colossians 4:7; 2 Timothy 4:12; Titus 3:12. (See Lightfoot on Colossians, the passage cited; B. D., under the word.)

STRONGS NT 5190a: υ [υ, Upsilon: on the use and the omission of the mark of diaeresis with, see Tdf. Prolog. p 108; Lipsius, Gram. Untersuch., p. 136ff; cf. Scrivener, Collation of Codex Sinaiticus, etc. 2nd edition, p. xxxviii.]
