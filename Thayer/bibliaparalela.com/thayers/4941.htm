STRONGS NT 4941: Συντύχη

Συντύχη and (so Tdf. editions 7, 8; cf. Lipsius, Gramm. Untersuch., p. 31; (Tdf. Proleg., p. 103; Kühner, § 84 at the end; on the other hand, Chandler § 199)) Συντύχη, ἡ (accusative: Συντύχην), Syntyche, a woman belonging to the church at Philippi: Philippians 4:2. (The name occurs several times its Greek inscriptions (see Lightfoot on Philippians, the passage cited).)
